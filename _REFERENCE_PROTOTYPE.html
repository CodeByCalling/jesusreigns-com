Our History

A Journey of 40 Years

1986

JRM Founded

Bishop Vincent and Dr. Ligaya Javier establish Jesus Reigns Ministries at 811 J. Nakpil St., Malate, Manila with a vision of a model church.

1990s

Provincial Expansion

JRM begins planting churches across the Philippines — from Luzon to Visayas to Mindanao. The DNG (Discipling the Next Generation) program takes root.

2000s

International Reach

JRM establishes outreaches in Hong Kong, Dubai, the United Kingdom, and other nations where Filipino communities are growing.

2010s

Institutional Growth

Jesus Reigns Christian Academy (JRCA) and Jesus Reigns Christian College (JRCC) are established under Dr. Ligaya Javier's leadership.

2026

40th Anniversary

JRM celebrates 40 years with 85 provincial churches, 55 ongoing missions, and a global digital home at jesusreigns.com.

Common Questions

You Might Be Wondering...

Where is JRM located?
We are at 811 J. Nakpil cor. P.H. Lim Sts., Malate, Manila 1004. You can also join us online every Sunday at 8:00 AM via YouTube and Facebook Live.
What time are the Sunday services?
We have three services every Sunday: 8:00 AM (also live-streamed), 10:30 AM, and 1:00 PM. You can come to whichever works best for you.
What should I wear?
Come as you are. Most people wear smart casual — comfortable and respectful. There is no dress code at JRM.
Is there anything for my kids?
Yes! We have ministry programs for children and youth. Ask our welcome team when you arrive and they'll guide you to the right place.
How can I get more connected?
The best first step is our DNG (Discipling the Next Generation) Program — a structured discipleship journey that has helped thousands of JRM members grow in their faith.
